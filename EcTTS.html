East Coast Table Top Studio


We originally started making games in 2015, with a small group of friends in high school and no official name for us. The group of friends who were on the team came and went, with varying levels of input and support with the original group halting operations in mid 2017. In November of 2020, the group was reformed under the name Ichor Labs with a potential release of a game in late 2021. Due to complications and delays, Ichor Labs disbanded, with the original project, Codex Breakerz, continuing development with the sole member of the original 2015 group. Shortly afterwards, select members of the original 2015 group reconnected and decided to join the development of Codex Breakerz. Shortly afterwards, in November 2021, formed East Coast Table Top Studio.
