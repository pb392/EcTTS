Codex Breakerz is a Collectable Card Game with the sole purpose of being a card game thatâ€™s easy to learn and get into, while possessing a high skill ceiling the longer the game is out. With a unique card design, players will get to play and build decks with some of the most interesting ratios card games have ever seen.


The game is built to be both fast and intricate. With only 30 cards in the deck, and 25 life, the game throws everything at the players from the start. Every card in the game, except the "Familiar Link Cards", are 2 cards in one, meaning when the player wants to play a card, they must decide between using the top effect or the bottom effect. Players also get to have special abilities depending on what Familiar Link card they choose to use. This is the first game to have mechanics like this, and is a very interesting game to play.
