Crown of Cinder is a game built off of a forgotten world lost to time. With the creation of many DnD campaigns and one-shots, those worlds are created with care and forgotten when we leave. Crown of Cinder takes these worlds and continues their story. Utilizing the mystical powers of Halcyon, players are able to continue their journey across the land as a Champion of the Nation, combining allies and spells to create the greatest fighting force the Forgotten Worlds have ever seen.

With a deck of 50 cards and a Champion Card any player can create a wide variety of intricate plays. The game's premise is to have a game where both players can play the exact same deck, but with the only difference being the Champion and the decks play completely different. Crown of Cinder took that premise and succeeded. All main deck cards are generic, with the exception of the Country Confidants, so players are able to use the same Halcyon Charge 2 card and have that card play 2 completely different roll in every deck. For one Champion it fuels their own mechanic while another Champion makes it into a draw 2 instead. With a very limited card pool to start with, and 4 of a kind per card, watch how every game unfolds in a unique and fascinating way.
